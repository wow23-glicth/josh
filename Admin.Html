About Admissions

Admission Requirements

A. Freshmen Enrollees:

  1. Form 138 (High School Card)
  2. Good moral certificate from high school principal
  3. Passed SMIT admission exam (if required)

B. Transferees:

  1. Honorable Dismissal
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. Transcript with grades and credits for evaluation

Conditional Admission

Students who failed three subjects last semester may be admitted conditionally under probationary status.

Refused Re-admission

  1. No academic improvement on probation
  2. Poor health
  3. Disciplinary issues
  4. Three subject failures last term
